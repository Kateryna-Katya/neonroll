The Game

How PlingoBall Works

Here's the basic concept behind PlingoBall:
balls are released from seashells and bounce through a field filled with obstacles, directions, and targets. Where the ball lands depends on the chosen setup and the path it takes — so every drop keeps you on edge.

  • the size of the field

  • the number of obstacles

  • the overall risk level
